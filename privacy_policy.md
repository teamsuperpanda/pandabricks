# Privacy Policy for Panda Bricks

*Last updated: 01-01-2025*

Team Super Panda ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Panda Bricks mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

## Information Collection and Use

Panda Bricks is designed to respect your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary for the App's core functionality:

### Data We Collect
- **Game Progress**: Local high scores and game settings

### Storage Location
- All game data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers

## Technical Implementation

### Local Storage
- High scores and settings are saved using SharedPreferences
- All data remains on your device
- Data can be cleared by uninstalling the App

### Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:
- Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates
- Just Audio: For game sound effects and music (no data collection)

## Children's Privacy

Our App is rated for all ages (ESRB E / PEGI 3) and complies with children's privacy protection laws. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to maintain the security of your local game data. Since all data is stored locally, the security of your information depends on your device's security settings.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the App accordingly.

## Your Rights

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data. You can:
- Clear all game data by uninstalling the App
- Manage app permissions through your device settings
